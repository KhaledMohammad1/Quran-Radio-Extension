# Privacy Policy for "إذاعة القرآن الكريم" (Quran Radio)

We take your privacy seriously. This extension:
- **Does NOT collect** any personal information or user data.
- **Does NOT track** your browsing history or activity.
- **Does NOT share** any data with third parties.

The extension only uses local storage to save your preferences (like volume level and theme) locally on your device.
